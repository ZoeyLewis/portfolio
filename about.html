About me

Basically, I like making cool things on screens.

Hey there, nice to virtually meet you! I'm Zoey, an aspiring front-end developer based in London.

Growing up, I always had a passion for technology and making beautiful things on screen. I spent hours of my youth designing graphics and editing photos on photoshop, and later on graduated with a BSc in Film Production Technology.

Since then, I've worked for production houses, media agencies and fintech startups, specialising in data.

My coding journey began through the SheCodes online workshops. SheCodes took my coding skills from zero to hero and I'm proud to have completed four projects, focusing on the use of React, APIs, responsive design, SEO and much more. I'm very excited to branch out and channel my creativity and skills into new projects. If you're interested in collaborating, please get in touch!
